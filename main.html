Roadhouse are a roots rock band from Wellington. We play a range of rootsy music from the genres of rock, blues, country, folk, R&B, rockabilly and the great American songbook.

Many of the songs we cover are from the '50s to the '90s, but our full repertoire spans more than 100 years of music.

Whether your event or venue calls for popular classics, interesting roots music, or a mix, we can put together a set to fit your needs. It can be also be all-electric, all-acoustic or both.

We're open to travelling outside the Wellington area.
