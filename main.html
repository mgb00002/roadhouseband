Roadhouse are a roots rock and roots music band based in the Wellington region.
They bring a mix of well-known and lesser known rock, folk, blues, country, classic
R&B, folk, rockabilly, and other songs from the great American songbook to the stage.
Their current repertoire spans quite a few decades; currently the 1930s to the 1990s.
