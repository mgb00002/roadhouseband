/* V 1.0 Site launch 22 June 2024 */ ROADHOUSEBAND

Roadhouse are a roots rock band based in Wellington. We play rock, blues, country, folk, standards, classic R&B and rockabilly.

Most of the songs we cover are from the '50s to the '90s, but our full repertoire spans nearly 100 years of music. We play both acoustic and electric music.

Whether your event needs popular classics, interesting roots music, or a mix, we can put together a set list to suit those needs.

Get in touch with us if you have any ideas in mind (we're also open to travelling outside of Wellington).
